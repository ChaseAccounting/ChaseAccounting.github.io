OpenAI Sora 2 Hits 25-Second Mark and Integrated "Talkies"

January 20, 2026

OpenAI has officially rolled out Sora 2, a massive update to their flagship video model. The most significant leap is the extension of clip length to a full 25 seconds for Pro users. More importantly, Sora has introduced synchronized audio generation.

The model generates high-fidelity video and audio simultaneously, including realistic lip-syncing and "environmental Foley." This allows for more coherent narratives without the "silent movie" feel of earlier versions.
