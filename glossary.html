Glossary of Agentic Finance (2026 Edition)

Agentic Synergy: A workflow where AI agents autonomously perform data synthesis (e.g., 100% transaction testing) while the human accountant provides final ethical sign-off.

Audit Trail Transparency: The specific ability of a "Reasoning Model" to document its logical steps in a way that satisfies GAAP and FASB regulatory standards.

Chain of Thought (CoT): The internal reasoning process of an AI. In accounting, verified CoT is required to prevent "Black Box" financial reporting.

Hallucinatory Hallmarks: Subtle mathematical or logical errors produced by non-reasoning AI models that require forensic verification.

Human-in-the-Loop (HITL): The mandatory oversight phase where a CPA or Finance lead verifies AI-generated journal entries before posting to the General Ledger.
